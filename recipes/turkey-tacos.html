Pineapple Turkey Tacos

Description

This is recipe I tried for the first time a couple of weeks ago. It's fast, healthy, and it's using up the rest of the red cabbage from the Sweetgreen Hot Honey Chicken Plate.

This is what the creator of the recipe had to say: Delicious and flavorful Pineapple Turkey Tacos, ready in 20 minutes for an easy weeknight meal. Add your favorite toppings, make into a taco salad if you wish! Gluten free and paleo friendly.

Ingredients

Pineapple Turkey Tacos

  • Oil for cooking
  • 1/2 medium yellow onion, chopped
  • ½ tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 pound ground turkey, 97% lean
  • 1 tablespoon homemade taco seasoning
  • ⅔ cup diced pineapple
  • ½ cup favorite jarred salsa
  • favorite taco shells

For Serving

  • sliced red cabbage
  • diced red bell pepper
  • shredded lettuce
  • sliced avocado
  • fresh cilantro
  • lime juice

Instructions

  1. Add oil to large skillet over medium heat. Add chopped onion and minced garlic; cook for about 3-4 minutes.
  2. Add ground turkey and taco seasoning, cooking until almost brown then add pineapple and salsa, simmer until meat is done.
  3. Make tacos with desired toppings. I like a little red cabbage, diced bell pepper, fresh pineapple, avocado slices, cilantro and some fresh lime.
